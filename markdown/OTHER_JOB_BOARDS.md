# Other Job Boards
The purpose of First Responder is to scrape lots of companies career sites and find the best jobs for you so that you don't have to do the manual searching yourself. I've found that this sucks when you try to scrape actual job boards like LinkedIn or Indeed. Those are better intended for using rather than scraping. Even though you will still be spending time searching job boards, you now don't have to go to individual company websites, as long as they're in the `companies` table!

Side note, the sites I'm about to mention are more tailored for people in the tech space, with the exception of LinkedIn. I am also not mentioning job boards like Indeed or Glassdoor because they suck!

## [LinkedIn](https://linkedin.com)
This is good for everyone! :)

## [Levels.fyi](https://www.levels.fyi/)
This is good for people looking for jobs that are tech related. Think software engineer, data engineer, data scientist, etc.

## [Builtin](https://builtin.com/)
I believe, don't quote me on this, that Builtin is to find jobs in specific cities. On top of that, it has a lot of jobs for startups and tech-related careers.

## [Simplify](https://simplify.jobs/)
Goated website to a degree. You can put in your resume and information about you and it will find jobs that match your skillset. I think this is probably the closest thing to First Responder as there is, because it's pretty good at showing you jobs you qualify for. They also have a browser extension that will let you fill in application pages from companies like Workday or Greenhouse so that you can go through them fast. That's how I found out about Simplify and would recommend!

They also have a [GitHub page](https://github.com/SimplifyJobs) that has some repositories that they update daily to show you jobs for specific things like [Summer 2026 Internships](https://github.com/SimplifyJobs/Summer2026-Internships) or [New Grad Positions](https://github.com/SimplifyJobs/New-Grad-Positions).

## [Wellfound](https://wellfound.com)
Wellfound has startup jobs, so if you're looking for startup jobs then this is a good site to check out!
